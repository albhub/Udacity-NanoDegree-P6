US Domestic Flights. 2003-2008
Conclusion.

Flights and delays. 1987-2008
The number of flights have increased and their "on time" arrival ratios.

Delay causes. 2003-2008
The main delay causes are the late-arriving aircraft, the carrier related, and the national aviation system ones. Two of them are under carrier control.
The main cause have changed during the years.

Airlines performance. 2003-2008
The late-arriving aircraft correlated with the total delayed time. The best carriers have improved the former to reduce the total delay.

The airlines could improve greatly their punctuality avoiding the late-arriving aircrafts and removing circumstances within the airline's control (e.g. maintenance or crew problems, aircraft cleaning, baggage loading, fueling, etc.).
